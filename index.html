Hex is a next-generation programming language.

Clean, simple, concise

hex has a clear, easy-to-understand syntax. Simple terminology like sput_oot or say_Tuxtz is common. The hxx compiler doesn't have complicated "flags" or options to use—just your input file and your output file!

Powerful

hex is built off of a powerful C++ core. C++ runs many of the world's applications, and has been proven to be easy to work with and understand.

Fast

hex enables you to write applications that operate at high speeds. Thanks to its powerful base, hex is the perfect choice for time-sensitive applications!
